Staples ensures your office, break room and warehouse spaces are fully stocked with the essentials that your business needs. What are Facility Supplies? Facility supplies include a variety of products, services and solutions to enable reliable maintenance and renovations for commercial facilities. Our selection of facility maintenance supplies includes everything you need for your office, school or place of business to be kept properly running and in its best possible condition year-round.
