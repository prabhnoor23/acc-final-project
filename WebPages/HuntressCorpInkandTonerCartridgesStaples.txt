High-Quality Outputs The Huntress Corp. printer cartridges offered by Staples offer page yields large enough to accommodate almost any office, even when fulfilling large print jobs. Industrial applications may require large quantities of printed material, and the quality must remain consistent throughout printing. Huntress Corp. ink cartridges and printer cartridges turn out fade-resistant documents and other media such as photos and images from the first page to the last. In addition to offering reliable production of media, documents and more, sealed Huntress Corp ink cartridges can be stored for long periods of time without drying out. MICR Security Huntress Corp. ink cartridges produce superior, secure printing results that are fade resistant. In addition, they are capable of magnetic ink character recognition, otherwise known as MICR security. MICR compatible ink cartridges and toner are suitable for secure check and bank printing as well as other financial record printing. For additional security, Huntress Corp. printer cartridges display a unique blue stain if the toner is tampered with. Find MICR black toner and compatible ink cartridges at Staples.
