No Chair Lumbar Support Fixed Chair Synchro Tilt Chairs with the synchro-tilt mechanism consist of a back and seat that move together in a 2-to-1 ratio. This means that for every two degrees that the back reclines, the seat will tilt back one degree. This allows the user’s feet to remain on the floor as they recline.
